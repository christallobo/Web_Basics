Founded as L&T Information Technology Ltd in December 1996, LTI is a wholly owned subsidiary of Larsen & Toubro Ltd (L&T). During 2001–2002 the company's name was changed from L&T Information Technology Limited to Larsen & Toubro Infotech Limited and in the same year the company achieved the assessed level of SEI Level 5. L&T Infotech dropped the word ‘Infotech’ from its name to reflect the changed business environment and rebranded itself as 'LTI' with a tag line of 'Let's Solve' in May 2017.
